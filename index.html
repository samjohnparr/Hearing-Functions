Hearing Functions

Hear math. See sound.

by Samuel John E. Parre√±o & Alleander Kate L. Gicale

This project lets students hear what a graph is doing. Each short clip scans from left to right and turns the graph into notes. Two mappings are used: V2P (value-to-pitch), in which pitch reflects height, and S2P (slope-to-pitch), in which pitch reflects slope (rate of change). The goal is to support visual understanding through sound, help learners notice patterns such as increasing and decreasing behavior and concavity, and provide an accessible way to explore functions.
